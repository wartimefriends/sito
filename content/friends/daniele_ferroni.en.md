Daniele Ferroni (1969), from Villanova di Bagnacavallo (Ravenna), is a photographer and micro-publisher who also occasionally writes short stories. In 2004, he founded Edizioni Lumacagolosa. He collaborates as a photographer with various newspapers and publishing houses, including Edizioni Pulcinoelefante, La Porta Nera, and Babbomorto Editore, for which he also directs the Babbogolosa series.
He loves photographing farmyard animals, poets, and friends.
